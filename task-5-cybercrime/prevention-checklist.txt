Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
Enable two factor authentication wherever possible
Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive information
Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments
Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
Avoid using public WiFi for financial transactions

UPI and Financial Safety

Double check UPI ID or phone number before sending money
Never approve unknown payment requests or collect requests
Do not scan random QR codes from unknown sources

Reporting Cybercrime

Report incidents at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
